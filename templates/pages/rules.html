Rules

Hand rankings (best to worst)

  1. Royal Flush — A K Q J 10 of the same suit
  2. Straight Flush — Five consecutive cards of the same suit
  3. Four of a Kind — Four cards of the same rank
  4. Full House — Three of a kind + a pair
  5. Flush — Five cards of the same suit
  6. Straight — Five consecutive cards
  7. Three of a Kind — Three cards of the same rank
  8. Two Pair — Two different pairs
  9. One Pair — Two cards of the same rank
  10. High Card — Highest card in hand

Order of play

  1. Dealer posts nothing. Cards are dealt starting left of the dealer.
  2. Small blind posts the small blind (player left of dealer).
  3. Big blind posts the big blind (player left of small blind).
  4. Pre-flop betting starts with the player left of big blind (under the gun), going clockwise.
  5. Post-flop betting starts with the small blind (or next active player left of dealer).

Betting rules

  • Minimum raise: at least the size of the previous raise in that round.
  • All-in: a player may go all-in for less than the minimum raise; others may re-raise.
  • Check is only allowed when no bet has been made in the current round.
